Secretary

Jigme Wangmo

A secretary plays a key role in ensuring smooth administrative operations within an organization.
They handle a range of responsibilities, including managing schedules, organizing meetings, preparing documents, and maintaining records.
Secretaries often act as the first point of contact, communicating on behalf of executives or departments, screening calls, and directing inquiries appropriately.
They coordinate travel arrangements, take meeting minutes, and assist with project coordination.
Additionally, secretaries manage filing systems, track deadlines, and ensure all information is accessible and organized.
