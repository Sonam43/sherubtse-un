Tree Plantation

Kinley Dorji

Sherubtse College organizes tree plantation events to promote sustainability and environmental awareness. Students, faculty, and community members plant trees on campus, helping improve biodiversity, reduce soil erosion, and enhance air quality. These efforts foster a greener campus and encourage a sense of responsibility toward nature.
