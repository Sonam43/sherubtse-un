Vice Coordinator

Avinash

The unit vice coordinator supports the unit coordinator in managing the daily operations of a team or department.
They assist with planning, organizing, and coordinating activities to ensure that tasks are completed effectively and on time.
The vice coordinator often steps in to lead meetings, manage schedules, and address issues in the coordinator’s absence.
They assist in organizing college events, providing resources for academic projects, and addressing student concerns to maintain a supportive academic experience. The vice coordinator also manages logistics, arranges meetings, and oversees documentation, ensuring compliance with institutional policies and regulations.
Additionally, the vice coordinator helps monitor team performance, report on progress, and encourage a positive work environment. Their role is essential in maintaining smooth operations and supporting the team’s overall success.
