Sherubste Students

The Advocacy Program for Sherubtse College, held at Womrong, serves as a platform to empower students and local community members with knowledge on pressing social issues, promote awareness, and foster community involvement. By inviting knowledgeable guest speakers, the program addresses topics like environmental conservation, public health, social justice, and community development, aligning with Bhutan’s commitment to Gross National Happiness. This program not only educates participants but also motivates them to contribute positively to their surroundings.
Guest speakers at the Womrong event are experts in their fields, providing insights and real-world perspectives on challenges that directly impact the local community. Through engaging presentations, interactive discussions, and hands-on activities, attendees gain a deeper understanding of their roles as active citizens. The advocacy program encourages students to adopt proactive stances on issues and empowers them to think critically, act responsibly, and inspire positive change in society.
The program’s impact extends beyond knowledge-sharing; it builds connections among participants, strengthens the community, and motivates students to become future leaders who will continue to advocate for the well-being of their communities.
