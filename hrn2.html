Blood Donation at Sherubtse

Sherubtse's Students

Sherubtse College students actively engage in blood donation drives to support the local community.
These events, often organized in collaboration with health organizations, see students volunteering to donate blood, demonstrating their commitment to saving lives and promoting health awareness.
The drives are organized on campus, making it convenient for students to participate between their academic schedules. Through these initiatives, students contribute to addressing blood shortages and assist hospitals in meeting emergency demands.
Their involvement not only provides critical support to patients in need but also fosters a spirit of compassion, responsibility, and unity within the Sherubtse community.
