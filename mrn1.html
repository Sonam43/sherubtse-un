Coordinator

Deki Tshomo

The Sherubtse College Coordinator plays a vital role in bridging the administrative, academic, and extracurricular functions of the college.
They oversee program coordination, manage scheduling, and ensure the smooth execution of various college activities, from events and workshops to academic programs.
Acting as a primary point of communication, the coordinator liaises between faculty, students, and administration, fostering an environment that supports student growth and community engagement.
They assist in organizing college events, providing resources for academic projects, and addressing student concerns to maintain a supportive academic experience. The coordinator also manages logistics, arranges meetings, and oversees documentation, ensuring compliance with institutional policies and regulations.
Additionally, they work closely with student clubs, like the Sherubtse UN Club, to enhance extracurricular learning and service opportunities.
