Ex-Coordinator

Namkhai Sellay

The ex-coordinator, or the former coordinator, once held a coordinating role but has since moved on or transitioned to a different position.
Their experience in managing team operations, setting goals, and overseeing project execution often makes them a valuable advisor or mentor for current coordinators.
They bring insights from past challenges, best practices, and lessons learned, which can help the team avoid pitfalls and improve efficiency.
Even after stepping down, an ex-coordinator might occasionally assist with training, provide guidance on strategic decisions, or offer support in times of transition.
Their past leadership experience and familiarity with the team’s goals and dynamics make them a respected resource, contributing to the unit’s continuity and long-term success.
