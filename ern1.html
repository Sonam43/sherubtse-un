Awareness at Kanglung primary school

Sherubste UN

In an effort to promote sustainable development and environmental consciousness, the Sherubtse United Nations Unit recently organized an awareness initiative at Kanglung Primary School. This program aimed to educate young minds about pressing global issues, including climate change, health, and the importance of community engagement.
Objectives of the Initiative
The primary goals of the awareness campaign were to:
Educate Students: Provide students with knowledge about global challenges and the role they can play in addressing these issues.
Promote Sustainable Practices: Encourage environmentally friendly practices, such as tree planting and recycling, to foster a culture of sustainability among the youth.
Encourage Active Participation: Inspire students to become active participants in their communities, contributing to local and global initiatives.
Activities Conducted
The initiative featured a range of engaging activities designed to capture the interest of the students:
Interactive Workshops: Facilitators conducted workshops focusing on topics like climate change and its impact on local ecosystems. These sessions included hands-on activities, allowing students to explore concepts in an engaging manner.
Art and Craft Sessions: Students participated in creative sessions where they designed posters and art pieces that conveyed messages about environmental protection and social responsibility.
Tree Planting Event: In collaboration with local authorities, the Sherubtse UN Unit organized a tree planting day. Students learned about the importance of trees in combating climate change while actively contributing to greening their school environment.
Discussion Forums: Open forums were held, allowing students to voice their thoughts and questions about global issues. This encouraged critical thinking and developed their ability to articulate concerns about their surroundings.
Impact and Future Prospects
The response from the students and faculty at Kunglung Primary School was overwhelmingly positive. Many students expressed newfound enthusiasm for environmental issues and a desire to take action within their community. The Sherubtse UN Unit plans to follow up on this initiative with regular activities and programs to sustain the momentum generated during the awareness campaign.
Through these efforts, the Sherubtse UN Unit is not only shaping informed and responsible future leaders but also fostering a strong sense of community involvement and environmental stewardship among the youth.
