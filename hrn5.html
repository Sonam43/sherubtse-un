The Sherubtse United Nations (UN) Club, based at Sherubtse College, serves as a dynamic platform for students interested in global issues, diplomacy, and international cooperation. With a commitment to promoting the principles and values of the United Nations, the club provides students with numerous opportunities to engage in meaningful discussions, simulations, and community projects that reflect pressing international challenges.
The club regularly organizes activities such as Model United Nations (MUN) simulations, where students assume roles as delegates from different countries to debate topics like human rights, environmental sustainability, and global peace. Through these experiences, members gain valuable insights into the workings of the UN and the complexities of diplomacy.
